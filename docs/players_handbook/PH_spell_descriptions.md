## SPELL EXPLANATIONS - SUMMARY DESCRIPTIONS

### CLERIC SPELLS

#### FIRST LEVEL

*   **Bless (Rev.):** Increases attack rolls and morale of friendly creatures by +1. **Reverse:** *Curse* decreases attack rolls and morale of enemy creatures by -1.
*   **Command:** Forces one creature to obey a single, clear, one-word command (e.g., *back, halt, flee*). Undead are not affected.
*   **Create Water (Rev.):** Generates 4 gallons of clean water per level of the caster. **Reverse:** *Destroy Water* obliterates a like quantity of water.
*   **Cure Light Wounds (Rev.):** Heals 1-8 points of damage to a creature by touch. **Reverse:** *Cause Light Wounds* inflicts 1-8 points of damage (requires touch attack).
*   **Detect Evil (Rev.):** Detects evil (or good for reverse) emanations from a creature or object in a 1" path up to 12".
*   **Detect Magic:** Detects magical radiations in a path up to 3" long. Blocked by thick stone or metal.
*   **Light (Rev.):** Creates a torch-light sphere (4" diameter) for 6 turns + 1 turn/level, or until extinguished. **Reverse:** *Darkness* creates a sphere of darkness for half the duration.
*   **Protection From Evil (Rev.):** Creates a magical armor around the recipient, preventing bodily contact by enchanted/conjured evil creatures, penalizing their "to hit" rolls by -2, and giving a +2 bonus to saving throws against their attacks.
*   **Purify Food & Drink (Rev.):** Makes up to 1 cubic foot of spoiled/poisoned food or water safe to consume. **Reverse:** *Putrefy Food & Drink* spoils safe food/water.
*   **Remove Fear (Rev.):** Instills courage, granting a +4 bonus to saving throws against magical fear for 1 turn. If affected by fear, grants another save with a +1/level bonus. **Reverse:** *Cause Fear* causes a victim to flee in panic.
*   **Resist Cold:** Creature touched is inured to cold, receiving a +3 bonus to saving throws and taking half (or quarter on successful save) damage from greater cold attacks (e.g., *ice storm*, dragon breath).
*   **Sanctuary:** Opponents must make a saving throw vs. magic to strike or attack the protected cleric. Cleric cannot take offensive action while protected.

#### SECOND LEVEL

*   **Augury:** Seeks to divine whether a future action (within 3 turns) will be beneficial or harmful. Base chance of 70% success + 1%/level.
*   **Chant:** Gives a +1 bonus to attack rolls, damage, and saving throws for friendly creatures within a 3" radius for as long as the cleric chants. Enemy rolls are penalized by -1.
*   **Detect Charm (Rev.):** Detects if a creature is under the influence of a *charm* spell (up to 10 creatures). **Reverse:** *Obscure Charm* protects a single creature from such detection.
*   **Find Traps:** Renders all hidden or magical traps (mechanical or magical) visible to the caster in a directional path.
*   **Hold Person (Charm):** Immobilizes 1-3 humans or humanoids (not including demi-humans like dwarves or elves) for a duration depending on the cleric's level. Saving throw is penalized based on the number of targets.
*   **Know Alignment (Rev.):** Reveals the exact alignment of up to 10 persons. **Reverse:** *Obscure Alignment* totally obscures the alignment of a single person for 1 turn.
*   **Resist Fire:** Creature touched is toughened to withstand heat, taking half (or quarter on successful save) damage from magical fires (e.g., *fireball*, dragon breath) and gaining a +3 bonus to saving throws vs. fire attacks.
*   **Silence, 15' Radius:** Creates a sphere of complete silence. Stops all conversation, spell casting, and noise. Can be cast on an object or unwilling creature (subject to save).
*   **Slow Poison:** Greatly slows the effects of venom, temporarily saving a victim up to 10 turns previous to casting. Victim loses 1 HP per turn but never drops below 1 HP while the spell lasts.
*   **Snake Charm:** Causes one or more snakes to cease activity and sway hypnotically. Total hit points of snakes affected cannot exceed the cleric's level.
*   **Speak With Animals:** Enables the cleric to communicate with any warm or cold-blooded animal (not mindless). Lasts 2 rounds per level.
*   **Spiritual Hammer (Evoc.):** Summons a field of force vaguely shaped like a hammer that strikes opponents within range as a magical weapon, with damage equal to a normal war hammer.

#### THIRD LEVEL

*   **Animate Dead:** Creates skeletons or zombies from dead human remains to obey the cleric's commands (1 monster/level).
*   **Continual Light (Rev.):** Creates a bright, permanent light (daylight equivalent). **Reverse:** *Continual Darkness* creates a permanent, impenetrable darkness.
*   **Create Food & Water:** Creates 1 cubic foot of nourishing food and/or water per level, sustaining three human-sized creatures for a full day.
*   **Cure Blindness (Rev.):** Permanently cures most forms of blindness by touch. **Reverse:** *Cause Blindness*.
*   **Cure Disease (Rev.):** Permanently cures most forms of disease by touch, making the creature whole in 1 turn to 1 week. **Reverse:** *Cause Disease* (requires touch attack and failed save).
*   **Dispel Magic:** Neutralizes magic it contacts. Base 50% chance of success, adjusted by the difference in caster levels (5% per level).
*   **Feign Death:** Puts the touched creature into a cataleptic state, appearing dead.
*   **Glyph of Warding (Evoc.):** Inscribes a magical rune to guard an area (25 sq. ft. per level) which, when triggered, releases a stored effect (e.g., electrical damage, fire damage, paralyze).
*   **Locate Object (Rev.):** Aids in locating a known or familiar object by direction. **Reverse:** *Obscure Object* hides an object from location spells.
*   **Prayer:** Duplicates the effects of *Chant* (friendly +1, enemy -1 to attack rolls, damage, and saving throws) but does not require continuous concentration.
*   **Remove Curse (Rev.):** Usually removes a curse on an object, person, or undesired presence. **Reverse:** *Bestow Curse* lowers one ability score to 3 (50% chance), or penalizes "to hit"/saves by -4 (25% chance), or causes object dropping (25% chance) for 1 turn/level (requires touch attack and failed save).
*   **Speak With Dead:** Allows the cleric to ask several questions of a dead creature (body or remains must be present). Limits on questions and maximum time dead depend on cleric level.
*   **Word of Recall:** Instantly teleports the cleric back to his or her previously designated sanctuary, carrying additional weight (250 gp/level) in equipment, treasure, or persons.

#### FOURTH LEVEL

*   **Cure Serious Wounds (Rev.):** Heals 3-17 (2d8+1) points of wound or injury damage. **Reverse:** *Cause Serious Wounds* inflicts 3-17 HP damage (requires touch attack).
*   **Detect Lie (Rev.):** Enables the recipient to determine if truth is being spoken (lasts 1 round/level). **Reverse:** *Undetectable Lie* makes a lie seem reasonable.
*   **Divination:** Gives information regarding an area (small woods, building, dungeon level). Success chance 60% + 1%/level, adjusted by DM for area specifics.
*   **Exorcise:** Negates possession of a creature or object by an outside/supernatural force (e.g., demon, curse, charm, *magic jar*). Base 1-100% chance of success, checked each turn.
*   **Lower Water (Rev.):** Causes water or similar fluid in a 1" x 1" square/level area to sink away (5% of original effect/level). **Reverse:** *Raise Water* causes water to return to its normal highest level + 1 foot/level.
*   **Neutralize Poison (Rev.):** Detoxifies any sort of venom in the creature or substance touched. Permanent only for existing poison. **Reverse:** *Poison* kills the victim if they fail a saving throw (requires touch attack).
*   **Protection From Evil, 10' Radius (Rev.):** Creates a larger protective globe (20' diameter sphere) identical in effect to *Protection From Evil*.
*   **Speak With Plants:** Enables the cleric to converse with any living vegetable, allowing questions about creatures, making thickets part, etc. (lasts 1 round/level).
*   **Sticks to Snakes (Rev.):** Changes 1 stick/level into a snake (5% chance/level of being venomous) that attacks as commanded. **Reverse:** *Snakes to Sticks* changes snakes to sticks.
*   **Tongues (Rev.):** Enables the cleric to speak the language of any creature inside the spell area. **Reverse:** *Confuse Languages* confuses verbal communication.

#### FIFTH LEVEL

*   **Atonement:** Removes the onus of unwilling or unknown deeds, or magical alignment change. Requires true repentance.
*   **Commune:** Contacts the cleric's divinity (or agents) to request information in the form of questions answered by a simple "yes" or "no" (1 question/level).
*   **Cure Critical Wounds (Rev.):** Heals 6-27 (3d8+3) HP damage. **Reverse:** *Cause Critical Wounds* inflicts 6-27 HP damage (requires touch attack).
*   **Dispel Evil (Rev.):** Causes summoned/enchanted evil creatures to return to their own plane or place. Affected creatures attack the caster at -7 penalty. **Reverse:** *Dispel Good* against summoned/enchanted good creatures.
*   **Flame Strike (Evoc.):** Calls down a column of fire, inflicting 6-48 (6d8) HP damage (half if saving throw is successful).
*   **Insect Plague:** Horde of insects swarms in a 36" diameter, 6" high cloud, dealing 1 HP damage/round to creatures within, and forcing morale checks.
*   **Plane Shift:** Moves the cleric (and up to 6 others linked in a circle) to another plane of existence. The specific destination depends on a small, forked metal rod component.
*   **Quest (Charm):** Requires an affected creature to perform a service and return with proof. Failure results in -1 penalty to saving throws daily.
*   **Raise Dead (Rev.):** Restores life to a dwarf, gnome, half-elf, halfling, or human dead up to 1 day per level of the cleric (subject to Constitution survival save). **Reverse:** *Slay Living* (inflicts *cause serious wounds* damage on a successful touch attack).
*   **True Seeing (Rev.):** Allows the cleric to see things as they actually are in a 12" sight range, revealing secret doors, invisible things, illusions, and creature auras. **Reverse:** *False Seeing* causes a person to see things as they are not.

#### SIXTH LEVEL

*   **Aerial Servant (Conj.):** Summons an invisible stalker to perform the cleric's bidding for a maximum of 1 day/level, primarily for finding and returning objects.
*   **Animate Object:** Imbues inanimate objects (1 cubic foot/level) with mobility and a semblance of life to attack as commanded.
*   **Blade Barrier (Evoc.):** Creates a wall of whirling, razor-sharp blades that inflicts 8-64 (8d8) HP damage to creatures passing through. Lasts 3 rounds/level.
*   **Conjure Animals:** Summons one or more mammals with total HD up to the cleric's level to attack opponents. Lasts 2 rounds/level.
*   **Find The Path (Rev.):** Enables the cleric to find the shortest, most direct route to a known destination (outdoors, underground, trap, or *maze* spell). **Reverse:** *Lose the Path* makes the creature touched totally lost.
*   **Heal (Rev.):** Completely cures any disease/blindness, and heals all wound/injury damage suffered (1-4 HP remaining damage). **Reverse:** *Harm* infects the victim with a disease and causes loss of all hit points (1-4 remaining damage).
*   **Part Water:** Causes water or similar liquid to move apart, forming a trough 3' deep by 1' wide by 2" long/level.
*   **Speak With Monsters:** Allows the cleric to converse with any type of creature with communicative ability (lasts 1 melee round/level).
*   **Stone Tell:** The very stones of an area (1 cubic yard of stone) speak and relate what has touched them, what is concealed, and what has transpired.
*   **Word Of Recall:** Instantly teleports the cleric and carried weight back to their sanctuary (same as 3rd level).

#### SEVENTH LEVEL

*   **Astral Spell:** Projects the cleric's astral body into the Astral Plane, leaving the physical body behind in suspended animation. Allows travel to other planes.
*   **Control Weather:** Allows the cleric to change the weather in an area of 4-16 square miles (takes 1 turn to cast, 1-4 turns for effect).
*   **Earthquake:** Creates a local tremor in a circular area (1/2" diameter/level), causing cliffs/caves to collapse, ground to crack, and structures to sustain damage/fall.
*   **Gate:** Opens an ultra-dimensional connection to a specific powerful being (demon, devil, demi-god, god, etc.), causing it to step through the portal or be attracted.
*   **Holy (Unholy) Word:** Utterance has tremendous power, driving off evil (or good for *Unholy Word*) creatures from other planes, and affecting other creatures of differing alignment (killing, paralyzing, stunning, deafening) based on their HD/level.
*   **Regenerate (Rev.):** Causes body members, bones, or organs to grow back. **Reverse:** *Wither* causes a member or organ to shrivel and cease functioning.
*   **Restoration (Rev.):** Raises the life energy level of the recipient creature by one, subsuming previous life energy level drain. **Reverse:** *Energy Drain* draws away one life energy level (requires touch attack).
*   **Resurrection (Rev.):** Restores life and complete strength to a dead person (subject to Constitution survival save) dead up to 10 years/level. **Reverse:** *Destruction* causes the victim to be instantly dead and turned to dust.
*   **Symbol (Conj.):** Inscribes magical runes that, when triggered, affect creatures passing over/touching/reading/passing through the portal with a chosen effect (Death, Discord, Fear, Hopelessness).
*   **Wind Walk:** Alters the cleric's body (and up to two others touched) to cloud-like vapors, allowing travel at up to 60" per turn (not invisible, but appear misty).

### DRUID SPELLS (Clerics)

*Descriptions for many Druid spells are noted as being identical to Cleric or Magic-User spells of the same name (e.g., *Detect Magic, Create Water, Cure Light Wounds*). Only unique effects are summarized.*

#### FIRST LEVEL

*   **Animal Friendship:** Charms one animal (non-semi-intelligent) to be a trusted friend and ally of the druid (subject to save). Allows druid to teach it 3 "tricks"/INT point.
*   **Detect Snares & Pits:** Detects hidden snares and pits (simple traps only underground, all traps outdoors) in a 1" path up to 4" long.
*   **Entangle:** Causes plants (*grasses, weeds, trees*) in a 4" diameter area to wrap, twist, and entwine creatures, holding them fast or slowing movement by 50% (subject to save).
*   **Faerie Fire:** Outlines an object or creature with a pale glowing light, making it visible (even if invisible) and giving a +2 penalty to opponents' AC ("to hit" bonus).
*   **Invisibility To Animals:** Makes the recipient totally undetectable by normal animals (INT under 6).
*   **Locate Animals:** Determines the direction and distance of any desired type of animal within range.
*   **Pass Without Trace:** Recipient moves through any terrain without leaving footprint or scent, making tracking impossible.
*   **Predict Weather:** Gains 100% accurate knowledge of the weather trend (sky, temperature, precipitation) for 2 hours in advance per level of the caster in a 9 square mile area.
*   **Shillelagh:** Changes a normal oaken club into a magical +1 weapon, inflicting 2-8 HP (man-sized) or 2-5 HP (larger).

#### SECOND LEVEL

*   **Barkskin:** Improves the recipient creature's Armor Class by 1 point and grants a +1 bonus to saving throws versus all attack forms except magic.
*   **Charm Person Or Mammal:** Charms one person or mammal (not monster) to regard the druid as a trusted friend and ally (subject to save). Duration depends on the charmed creature's intelligence.
*   **Fire Trap (Evoc.):** Traps a closable object (e.g., door, chest) to explode with fire damage (1-4 HP, half with save) when opened/touched by an unauthorized creature.
*   **Heat Metal (Rev.):** Causes ferrous metal (iron, steel) in a mass of 500 gp/level to become searing hot over 7 rounds, causing damage and disability to exposed flesh. **Reverse:** *Chill Metal* causes metal to become freezing, causing damage and amputation.
*   **Obscurement:** Causes a misty vapor to arise around the druid, reducing visibility to 2' to 8' (2d4) for 4 rounds per level.
*   **Produce Flame:** Creates a bright, hot flame in the druid's palm. Lasts 2 rounds/level. Can be hurled as a missile (range 4"), igniting combustibles.
*   **Trip (Charm):** Causes a stick, vine, or rope object to rise and trip creatures crossing it (subject to save). Creatures running take damage and are stunned.
*   **Warp Wood:** Permanently bends and warps wood in a 1" range/level, destroying its straightness, form, and strength.

#### THIRD LEVEL

*   **Call Lightning (Evoc.):** Calls down bolts of lightning from the sky (must be a storm) that inflict 2d8+1d8/level HP damage (half with save) to creatures within a 1" radius of the strike.
*   **Hold Animal (Charm):** Holds 1-4 animals rigid (mammals, birds, or reptiles, not monsters) for 2 rounds/level. Saving throw is penalized based on the number of targets.
*   **Plant Growth:** Causes normal vegetation (brush, trees, vines) in a 2" X 2" square area/level to become a dense barrier.
*   **Protection From Fire:** Grants invulnerability to normal fires and 50% damage reduction (+4 save bonus) against magical fires for 1 turn/level.
*   **Pyrotechnics:** Causes a fire source to produce either a flashing burst of fireworks (blinding creatures) or a thick stream of smoke (obscuring vision).
*   **Snare (Charm):** Creates a 90% undetectable snare from a flexible object (vine, rope) that contracts to bind or strangle a creature (1-6 HP damage) when triggered.
*   **Stone Shape:** Permanently forms an existing piece of stone (3 cubic feet/level) into a desired shape (e.g., weapon, trapdoor, idol).
*   **Summon Insects:** Attracts a cloud of flying or crawling insects (70% chance of flying) that swarm and attack a target, dealing 2 HP damage/round, and forcing the victim to fend them off.
*   **Tree:** Allows the druid to assume the form of a small living tree or shrub, or a large dead tree trunk, and observe their surroundings while in the form.
*   **Water Breathing (Rev.):** Allows the recipient to freely breathe underwater for 6 turns/level. **Reverse:** *Air Breathing* allows water-breathing creatures to survive in the atmosphere.

#### FOURTH LEVEL

*   **Animal Summoning I:** Calls up to eight animals (max 4 HD each) of whatever sort the druid names (if within range) to aid the druid.
*   **Call Woodland Beings:** Summons certain woodland creatures (brownies, centaurs, dryads, pixies, satyrs, sprites, treants, unicorns) to the druid's locale to aid them (subject to save).
*   **Control Temperature, 10' Radius:** Alters the temperature surrounding the druid by 9 degrees Fahrenheit per level (up or down).
*   **Hallucinatory Forest (Rev.):** Creates the illusion of a perfectly natural forest in a 4" square/level area. **Reverse:** *Hallucinatory Clearing*.
*   **Hold Plant (Charm):** Affects vegetable matter (e.g., green slime, treants) to cease moving, entwining, or making sound.
*   **Plant Door:** Opens a magical portal through trees or other growth for the druid (and higher level druids/dryads) to enter and move from inside to another tree of the same species.
*   **Produce Fire (Rev.):** Causes a common-type fire (up to 12" square) to ignite combustibles and cause 1-4 HP damage in a single round. **Reverse:** *Quench Fire* extinguishes a normal fire.
*   **Protection From Lightning:** Grants invulnerability to normal electrical attacks and 50% damage reduction (+4 save bonus) against magical lightning/electrical attacks for 1 turn/level.
*   **Repel Insects:** Creates an invisible barrier up to 10' radius that prevents normal insects from approaching.

#### FIFTH LEVEL

*   **Animal Growth (Rev.):** Causes animals (max 8) in a 2" square area to grow to twice their normal size, doubling hit dice and damage in combat. **Reverse:** *Animal Shrinkage* reduces size by half.
*   **Animal Summoning II:** Calls up to six animals (max 8 HD each) or 12 animals (max 4 HD each).
*   **Anti-Plant Shell:** Creates an invisible hemispherical force field (20' diameter) that prevents the entrance of any animal matter of normal (not magical) nature.
*   **Commune With Nature:** Enables the druid to become one with nature in a 1/2 mile radius/level, gaining knowledge of one fact about the surrounding territory (ground, plants, water, people).
*   **Control Winds:** Alters wind force in a 4"/level radius hemisphere, increasing/decreasing force by 3 mph/level (e.g., winds of 75+ mph cause devastation).
*   **Moonbeam:** Summons a 1" diameter, 1" long/level column of pale, cold light that inflicts 2-12 (2d6) HP damage (half with save) to creatures within and has an effect similar to *Dispel Magic* on enchanted creatures.
*   **Pass Plant:** Allows the druid to enter a tree and move from inside it to another living tree of the same species within range.
*   **Spike Stones:** Permanently creates an area of jagged spikes (1" square area/level) that inflicts 2-12 (2d6) HP damage to creatures moving 1" or more per round.
*   **Transmute Rock to Mud (Rev.):** Turns natural rock (2 cubic "/level) into an equal volume of mud. **Reverse:** *Mud to Rock* hardens normal mud into soft stone.
*   **Wall of Fire (Evoc.):** Creates a blazing curtain of magical fire (up to 2" square/level) that inflicts 4-16 HP damage (or 2-8 HP near the wall) to creatures passing through.

#### SIXTH LEVEL

*   **Animal Summoning III:** Calls up to four animals (max 16 HD each), eight animals (max 8 HD each), or 16 creatures (max 4 HD each).
*   **Anti-Animal Shell:** Creates an invisible hemispherical force field (20' diameter) that prevents the entrance of any animal matter of normal (not magical) nature.
*   **Conjure Fire Elemental (Rev.):** Summons a strong (16 HD) fire elemental to aid the druid. **Reverse:** *Dismiss Fire Elemental*.
*   **Feeblemind (Charm):** Causes a victim's brain to become that of a moronic child, remaining in this state until *heal*, *restoration*, or *wish* is used.
*   **Fire Seeds (Conj.):** Creates up to four acorn missiles (2-16 HP on impact) or eight holly berry timed incendiaries (1-8 HP burst damage on command) that ignite combustibles.
*   **Liveoak:** Permanently animates an acorn into a Small, mobile tree to serve as a guard or servitor.
*   **Transport Via Plants:** Allows the druid to enter any large living plant and pass any distance to a living plant of the same species in a single round.
*   **Turn Wood:** Pushes away all wooden objects in a 12" wide path, 2" long/level, splinters fixed objects, and drags objects (and carriers) back.
*   **Wall Of Thorns (Conj.):** Creates a barrier of very tough, pliable green tangled brush (10" cube/level) that inflicts 8 HP damage + AC to creatures breaking through.
*   **Weather Summoning:** Calls forth weather commensurate with the climate and season (e.g., tornado, heat wave, blizzard). Not under the druid's control.

#### SEVENTH LEVEL

*   **Animate Rock:** Causes a single lithic object (2 cubic feet of stone/level) to move and attack as commanded.
*   **Chariot Of Sustarre (Evoc.):** Summons a large flaming chariot pulled by two fiery horses for transport (24" on ground, 48" flying). Creatures within 5' take *wall of fire* damage.
*   **Confusion (Charm):** Causes 2-8 creatures within a 4" X 4" area to become confused (act randomly, 10% wander away, 50% confused, 20% attack nearest, 20% attack druid/party).
*   **Conjure Earth Elemental (Rev.):** Summons a strong (16 HD) earth elemental to aid the druid. **Reverse:** *Dismiss Earth Elemental*.
*   **Creeping Doom (Conj.):** Calls forth a mass of venomous, biting, and stinging arachnids, insects, and myriapods (500-1000 in number) that swarm and attack.
*   **Finger Of Death (Charm):** Causes the victim's heart to stop, slaying the creature if it fails its saving throw.
*   **Fire Storm (Rev.):** Creates a whole area (2" cube/level) shot through with sheets of roaring flame, inflicting 2-16 HP damage + 1 HP/level (half with save). **Reverse:** *Fire Quench* smothers a fire.
*   **Reincarnate:** Brings back a dead person (dead up to 1 week) in a new, random body and race (subject to *Magic-User Reincarnation Table*).
*   **Transmute Metal To Wood:** Changes one metal object (max 80 gp weight/level) into wood. Magical objects are 10% likely to be affected.

### MAGIC-USER SPELLS

#### FIRST LEVEL

*   **Affect Normal Fires:** Causes small fires (up to 3' diameter) to reduce in size/light (half consumption) or increase in size/light (double consumption). Heat output is unchanged.
*   **Burning Hands:** Sends a fan-like sheet of searing flame (3' long, 120° arc) from fingertips, inflicting 1 HP damage/level to any creature in the area (no save).
*   **Charm Person:** Charms one person (human/humanoid, not demi-human) to regard the caster as a trusted friend and ally.
*   **Comprehend Languages (Rev.):** Allows the caster to read any incomprehensible written message (not magical writing) or understand the language of a speaking creature by touch.
*   **Dancing Lights:** Creates 1-4 lights resembling torches, spheres, or a vague man-like shape, that move as the caster desires.
*   **Enlarge (Rev.):** Increases a living creature (up to 200% max) or object (up to 100% max) size and weight by 20% per level. **Reverse:** *Reduce* decreases size.
*   **Erase:** Removes writings (magical or mundane) from a scroll or two facing pages (50% base chance + 2%/level for magical, +4%/level for mundane).
*   **Feather Fall:** Instantly changes the rate of fall for creature(s)/objects to 2' per second (12' per segment), preventing damage on landing.
*   **Find Familiar (Conj.):** Summons an abnormally intelligent animal familiar (e.g., cat, hawk, owl) to aid the magic-user.
*   **Friends (Charm):** Causes the magic-user to gain a temporary increase of 2-8 points in Charisma, or a temporary lowering of 1-4 points (subject to save by creatures in the area).
*   **Hold Portal:** Magically bars a door, gate, or valve for 1 round/level. Cannot be used on *wizard locked* doors.
*   **Identify:** Reveals 1 property of a touched magical item (requires pearl/owl feather component, and Constitution check upon use).
*   **Jump:** Empowers the recipient to leap up to 30' forward, 10' backward, or straight upward.
*   **Magic Missile (Evoc.):** Creates 1-5 magical missiles that unerringly strike a target, each dealing 2-5 (1d4+1) HP damage. (Gains missiles at 3rd, 5th, 7th levels, etc.).
*   **Mending:** Repairs small breaks in non-magical objects (broken ring, hole in leather sack, etc.).
*   **Message:** Whispers a message to a pointed creature that can reply in a straight line.
*   **Nystul's Magic Aura (Phantasm):** Gives one item a false magical aura to fool *detect magic*.
*   **Push (Conj.):** Causes an invisible force (1 foot-pound/level) to strike an object, moving small objects or causing a creature to lose its balance.
*   **Shield (Evoc.):** Creates an invisible barrier that provides AC 2 vs. hurled missiles, AC 3 vs. small device-propelled missiles, and AC 4 vs. all others. Also negates *magic missile*.
*   **Shocking Grasp:** Develops a powerful electrical charge in the caster's hand, inflicting 1-8 HP damage + 1 HP/level to the creature touched.
*   **Sleep (Charm):** Causes 1-16 HD of creatures (up to 4+4 HD) in a 3" diameter circle to fall into a comatose slumber.
*   **Spider Climb:** Enables the recipient to climb and travel on vertical surfaces or ceilings at 3" movement rate.
*   **Tenser's Floating Disc (Evoc.):** Creates a 3' diameter, circular plane of null-gravity that holds 1,000 gp weight/level and follows the caster.
*   **Ventriloquism (Phantasm):** Makes the caster's voice (or similar sound) appear to issue from someplace else (another creature, statue, behind a door).

#### SECOND LEVEL

*   **Darkness, 15' Radius:** Creates a total, impenetrable darkness in a 15' radius sphere.
*   **Detect Invisibility:** Clearly sees any objects or creatures that are invisible (or astral, ethereal, hidden, or out of phase).
*   **ESP:** Detects the surface thoughts of any creature in range (except undead and those with no mind). Range and number of probes increase with level.
*   **Fools Gold:** Temporarily changes copper coins to gold pieces, or brass items to solid gold. Chance of cold-wrought iron dispelling the dweomer (25-30% base chance).
*   **Forget (Charm):** Causes 1-4 individual creatures within a 2" X 2" area to forget the events of the previous round.
*   **Knock:** Magically opens stuck, held, or *wizard locked* doors, locked/barred doors, and locked/trick opening boxes or chests.
*   **Leomund's Trap (Phantasm):** Places a false trap illusion upon a small mechanism (lock, hinge) designed to fool dwarves and thieves (80% likely to be seen).
*   **Levitate:** Allows the caster or another creature (up to 1,000 gp weight/level) to move vertically at 20' per round (caster) or 10' per round (other).
*   **Locate Object (Rev.):** Aids in location of a desired object, with range 2"/level.
*   **Magic Mouth:** Empowers an object (not living/intelligent) with an enchanted mouth that speaks a 25-word message when a specified event occurs.
*   **Mirror Image (Phantasm):** Creates 2-5 exact, moving duplicates of the caster that disappear when struck by an attack.
*   **Misdirection (Phantasm):** Misdirects the information from a detection-type spell (e.g., *detect magic, detect evil*) about a specific creature or item.
*   **Phantasmal Force (Phantasm):** Creates a visual illusion of an object, creature, or force (4 square" + 1 square"/level). Creatures believe it and can suffer damage until they successfully disbelieve.
*   **Pyrotechnics:** Causes a fire source to produce either blinding fireworks (lasts 1 segment/level) or a choking cloud of smoke (lasts 1 round/level, totally obscures vision).
*   **Ray Of Enfeeblement (Charm):** Weakens an opponent, reducing Strength by 25% + 2%/level (subject to save).
*   **Rope Trick:** Causes one end of a 5'-30' rope to rise into the air and anchor in an extra-dimensional space, allowing the caster and up to five others to climb to safety.
*   **Scare (Charm):** Causes a creature with < 6+1 HD/levels to fall into a fit of trembling and shaking (subject to save).
*   **Shatter:** Shivers non-magical objects of crystal, glass, ceramic, or porcelain (up to 100 gp/level) into dozens of pieces.
*   **Stinking Cloud (Evoc.):** Creates a billowing cloud of nauseous vapors (2" X 2" X 2" cloud) that causes creatures to be helpless due to nausea (or half helpless on successful save).
*   **Strength:** Increases a creature's Strength ability score (1-6 points or 1-8 tenths of 18) for 6 turns/level.
*   **Web (Evoc.):** Creates a sticky mass of tough, flammable strands (max 8 cubic inches) that traps creatures with < 13 STR. Webs are flammable, dealing 2-8 HP damage when burnt.

#### THIRD LEVEL

*   **Blink:** Causes the caster's material form to "blink" out and back to the plane in a random period/direction each round. Caster is only 75% likely to perform other actions during this spell.
*   **Clairaudience:** Allows the caster to concentrate on a known locale and hear in their mind whatever noise is within a 6" radius of that locale center.
*   **Clairvoyance:** Allows the caster to see in their mind whatever is within sight range (normal or infrared/ultraviolet) of a known locale.
*   **Dispel Magic:** Neutralizes magic it contacts (same as Cleric 3rd Level).
*   **Explosive Runes:** Traces mystic runes on a book/map/scroll that prevent unauthorized reading. When read, the runes detonate, dealing 12-30 (6d4+6) HP damage (no save).
*   **Fireball (Evoc.):** Creates an explosive burst of flame (2" radius sphere) that deals 1d6 HP damage/level (half if saving throw is successful).
*   **Flame Arrow (Conj.):** Touches one arrow or crossbow bolt (quarrel) per segment, making it magical and causing +1 HP additional fire damage on a successful hit.
*   **Fly:** Bestows the power of magical flight (12" vertical/horizontal, 24" dive/ascend) for 1 turn/level + 1-6 turns.
*   **Gust Of Wind:** Creates a strong puff of air (1" wide path, 1"/level long) that extinguishes small flames, fans large fires, forces back small flying creatures, and slows large flying creatures by 50%.
*   **Haste (Rev.):** Causes affected creatures (1 creature/level in a 4" X 4" area) to function at double their normal movement and attack rates for 3 rounds + 1 round/level. **Reverse:** *Slow* causes the opposite effect.
*   **Hold Person (Charm):** Immobilizes 1-4 persons (human/humanoid, including demi-humans) for 2 rounds/level.
*   **Infravision:** Enables the recipient to see in the infrared spectrum up to 60' (lasts 12 turns + 6 turns/level).
*   **Invisibility, 10' Radius (Phantasm):** Makes the creature touched and all its gear within a 10' radius invisible.
*   **Leomund's Tiny Hut:** Creates an opaque sphere of force (10' diameter, half above/half below ground) that maintains 70°F inside temperature and withstands up to 50 mph winds.
*   **Lightning Bolt (Evoc.):** Releases a powerful stroke of electrical energy (forking bolt 1" wide, 4" long, or single bolt 1/2" wide, 8" long) that deals 1d6 HP damage/level (half if saving throw is successful).
*   **Monster Summoning I (Conj.):** Summons 1-4 first level monsters to attack the caster's opponents (lasts 2 rounds + 1 round/level).
*   **Phantasmal Force (Phantasm):** Creates a visual illusion (same as 2nd level Improved, but larger area) which can inflict damage until disbelieved.
*   **Protection From Evil, 10' Radius (Rev.):** Creates a larger protective globe identical in effect to *Protection From Evil*.
*   **Protection From Normal Missiles:** Bestows total invulnerability to hurled and projected missiles (arrows, axes, darts, etc.) and reduces damage from large/magical missiles.
*   **Suggestion (Charm):** Influences the actions of one creature by uttering a few words suggesting a reasonable course of action (lasts 6 turns + 6 turns/level).
*   **Tongues (Rev.):** Enables the caster to speak the language of any creature (same as Cleric 4th Level).
*   **Water Breathing (Rev.):** Allows the recipient to freely breathe underwater for 3 turns/level.

#### FOURTH LEVEL

*   **Charm Monster (Charm):** Charms any living creature (or several of lesser level) to regard the caster as friendly (same as 1st level, but more powerful).
*   **Confusion (Charm):** Causes creatures in a 4" X 4" area to become confused (same as Druid 7th Level).
*   **Dig (Evoc.):** Excavates 125 cubic feet of earth/sand/mud per round (5' cube).
*   **Dimension Door:** Instantly transfers the caster and additional weight (up to 5,000 gp) to a well-known destination within range (no error).
*   **Enchanted Weapon (Rev.):** Turns an ordinary weapon (two small or one large) into a magical weapon (no bonuses, but hits any opponent) for 5 rounds/level.
*   **Extension I:** Prolongs the duration of a previously cast 1st, 2nd, or 3rd level spell by 50%.
*   **Fear (Phantasm):** Sends forth an invisible ray that causes creatures (HD 1 to 12) within its area to turn and flee in panic (base 60% chance, reduced 5%/level).
*   **Fire Charm (Charm):** Causes a normal fire source to serve as a magical agent, with a gossamer veil of flame circling it, charming creatures observing it to remain motionless (subject to save).
*   **Fire Shield (Evoc.):** Immolates the caster (but no damage taken) with flames, protecting against cold-based attacks (half damage, +2 save bonus) and dealing double damage to body/hand-held weapon attackers.
*   **Fire Trap (Evoc.):** Same as Druid 2nd Level, but explosion deals 1-4 HP damage + 1 HP/level.
*   **Fumble (Charm):** Causes the recipient creature to suddenly become clumsy and awkward, potentially dropping held objects or weapons.
*   **Hallucinatory Terrain (Phantasm):** Creates an illusion which hides the actual terrain (e.g., field looks like a swamp) in an area 1" X 1" square/level.
*   **Ice Storm (Evoc.):** Causes either great hail stones (3-30 HP damage) or driving sleet (blinding creatures and slowing movement by 50%) to fall in the area of effect.
*   **Massmorph (Phantasm):** Causes willing creatures (man-size or smaller, up to 10/level) to appear as normal trees of any sort.
*   **Minor Globe of Invulnerability (Abj.):** Creates a magical sphere around the caster which prevents any 1st, 2nd, or 3rd level spells from penetrating.
*   **Monster Summoning II (Conj.):** Summons 1-6 second level monsters.
*   **Polymorph Other:** Completely alters the form, ability, personality, and mentality of one creature (subject to Constitution system shock save).
*   **Polymorph Self:** Allows the caster to assume the form of any creature (wren to hippopotomus) and its form of locomotion, but without gaining new abilities (2 turns/level).
*   **Rary's Mnemonic Enhancer:** Allows the magic-user to memorize (or retain the memory of) three additional spell levels (e.g., three 1st level spells, or one 3rd level spell).
*   **Remove Curse (Rev.):** Same as Cleric 3rd Level.
*   **Wall Of Fire (Evoc.):** Creates a vertical wall of shimmering magical fire (30' long/level, 20' high/level) that inflicts 2-12 HP damage (or 5-30 HP near the wall).
*   **Wall Of Ice (Evoc.):** Creates a sheet of strong, flexible ice (1" thick/level, 1" square area/level) that stops pursuers and deals 2 HP damage/inch of thickness to creatures breaking through.
*   **Wizard Eye (Alt.):** Creates an invisible sensory organ that sends visual information to the caster and travels at 3" per round.

#### FIFTH LEVEL

*   **Airy Water:** Turns normal liquid to a less dense, breathable substance in a 2" diameter sphere/4" diameter hemisphere, allowing movement and breathing underwater.
*   **Bigby's Interposing Hand (Evoc.):** Creates a large, disembodied hand of force that interposes itself between the caster and opponent, taking damage as the caster.
*   **Chaos (Charm):** Causes all creatures in a 4" X 4" area to become confused (same as 4th level, but affects all creatures).
*   **Cloudkill (Evoc.):** Generates a billowing cloud of yellowish-green vapors (4" wide, 2" high, 2" deep) that slays creatures with < 4+1 HD/levels, and causes those up to 6 HD/levels to save vs. poison or be slain.
*   **Cone Of Cold (Evoc.):** Causes a cone-shaped area of extreme cold (1/2" long/level) to erupt from the caster's hand, inflicting 1d4+1 HP damage/level (half if saving throw is successful).
*   **Conjure Elemental:** Summons a 16 HD Air, Earth, Fire, or Water Elemental to do the caster's bidding (requires concentration, 5% chance of turning on caster).
*   **Contact Other Plane (Div.):** Sends the caster's mind to another plane of existence to request information (answered with "yes/no/maybe/never"). Risk of insanity or death increases with distance.
*   **Distance Distortion:** Causes an earth elemental to distort an area's dimensions (100 sq. "/level) to effectively appear half or twice the distance to those traveling over it.
*   **Feeblemind (Charm):** Causes the victim's brain to become that of a moronic child (same as Druid 6th Level).
*   **Hold Monster (Charm):** Immobilizes 1-4 creatures of any type (monsters/humans) in sight and range for 1 round/level.
*   **Leomund's Secret Chest (Alt., Conj.):** Instantly transfers a specially crafted chest and its contents (1 cubic foot/level) to the ethereal plane, retrievable by a tiny replica.
*   **Magic Jar (Poss.):** Transfers the caster's life force to a special container (gem/crystal), from which it can possess the mind and body of a victim (subject to save).
*   **Monster Summoning III (Conj.):** Summons 1-4 third level monsters.
*   **Mordenkainen's Faithful Hound (Conj.):** Summons a phantom watchdog that only the caster can see. It guards an area and attacks creatures approaching it from behind with a vicious 3-18 HP bite.
*   **Passwall:** Opens a passage (5' wide, 8' high, 10' deep) through wooden, plaster, or stone walls. Lasts 6 turns + 1 turn/level.
*   **Stone Shape:** Same as Druid 3rd Level.
*   **Telekinesis:** Mentally moves objects or opponents (up to 250 gp weight/level) by will force, with movement increasing each round.
*   **Teleport:** Instantly transports the caster and additional weight to a well-known destination (distance is not a factor). Risk of error increases with unfamiliarity.
*   **Transmute Rock To Mud (Rev.):** Same as Druid 5th Level.
*   **Wall Of Force (Evoc.):** Creates an invisible barrier (20' square/level) that is totally impervious to all magic, spells, blows, and missiles (destroyed only by *disintegrate*).
*   **Wall Of Iron (Evoc.):** Creates a vertical iron wall (1/4" thick/level, 15 sq. ft./level) that inserts itself into surrounding material. Permanent unless dispelled or destroyed by force.
*   **Wall Of Stone (Evoc.):** Creates a wall of granite rock (1/4" thick, 20' square/level) that merges into adjoining rock surfaces. Permanent unless destroyed by *disintegrate* or force.

#### SIXTH LEVEL

*   **Anti-Magic Shell (Abj.):** Creates an invisible barrier (1'/level diameter sphere) that surrounds the caster and moves with them, totally impervious to all magic and spell effects (1st-5th level spells).
*   **Bigby's Forceful Hand (Evoc.):** Creates a large, disembodied hand of force that pushes creatures (up to 500 lbs) away or slows movement of larger creatures.
*   **Death Spell (Conj.):** Instantly and irrevocably slays creatures with less than 2 HD, or 4-80 (4d20) HD of creatures with 2-8 HD in a 1/2" square/level area.
*   **Disintegrate (Alt.):** Causes matter (living or non-living, up to 1" cubic volume) to vanish permanently (subject to save).
*   **Enchant An Item (Conj.):** Prepares a non-magical item (must be finest quality) to accept a magical dweomer. Casting time is 16 hours + 8d8 hours.
*   **Extension III:** Prolongs the duration of a previously cast 1st-3rd level spell to double duration, and 4th or 5th level spell by 50%.
*   **Geas (Charm):** Places a magical command upon a creature to carry out some service or refrain from some action. Failure results in sickness and eventual death (1-4 weeks).
*   **Glassee:** Makes a section of metal, stone, or wood transparent to the caster's gaze (or creates a transparent area) for 1 round/level.
*   **Globe Of Invulnerability (Abj.):** Creates a magical sphere around the caster which prevents any 1st through 4th level spells from penetrating.
*   **Guards And Wards (Evoc.):** Defends a stronghold with various permanent effects: misty corridors, *wizard locked* doors, illusory wall, web-filled stairs, minor confusion, and other chosen magics.
*   **Invisible Stalker (Conj.):** Summons an 8 HD invisible stalker from the Elemental Plane of Air to obey the caster's commands.
*   **Legend Lore (Div.):** Determines information regarding a known person, place, or thing (casting time 1 turn up to 12 weeks, depending on information known).
*   **Move Earth:** Moves dirt (clay, loam, sand) and its components to collapse embankments, shift dunes, etc., over a 4" square area/turn of casting.
*   **Otiluke's Freezing Sphere (Evoc.):** Creates a globe of matter at absolute zero temperature that, on contact with water, freezes a 6" deep layer of ice over 100 sq. ft./level, or can be cast as a thin ray (4 HP/level damage).
*   **Project Image (Phantasm):** Creates a non-material duplicate of the caster up to 1"/level distant, which performs actions (walking, speaking, spell-casting) identical to the caster. Spells originate from the image.
*   **Reincarnation:** Same as Druid 7th Level (but with *Magic-User Reincarnation Table*).
*   **Repulsion (Abj.):** Causes all creatures in a 1" path to move away from the caster at 3" per round.
*   **Spiritwrack (Evoc.):** Strong protection/punishment spell against powerful creatures of the nether planes (Demons, Devils) whose name the caster knows. Roots them to the spot and causes pain/damage unless they succeed in a series of saves.
*   **Stone To Flesh (Rev.):** Turns any sort of stone (9 cubic feet/level) into flesh (restoring life if formerly living). **Reverse:** *Flesh to Stone* turns flesh of any sort to stone (subject to save).
*   **Tenser's Transformation (Evoc.):** Undergoes a startling transformation, doubling hit points and attack rate, improving AC by 4, and granting +2 damage on attacks (lasts 1 round/level).

#### SEVENTH LEVEL

*   **Bigby's Grasping Hand (Evoc.):** Creates a huge, disembodied hand of force that can hold motionless a creature or object of up to 1,000 lbs weight.
*   **Cacodemon (Conj.):** Summons a powerful Demon of type IV, V, or VI (if name is known) to serve the caster (by threat of *spiritwrack* or promise of tribute/human sacrifices).
*   **Charm Plants (Charm):** Brings under command vegetable life forms in a 3" X 1" area/level (same as Druid 7th Level).
*   **Delayed Blast Fireball (Evoc.):** Same as *Fireball*, but +1 on each damage die, and can be commanded to release its blast up to 5 rounds after casting.
*   **Drawmij's Instant Summons (Conj.):** Instantly teleports one small, non-living object (max 75 gp weight) to the caster's hand upon crushing a gem component.
*   **Duo-Dimension:** Causes the caster to have only two dimensions, making them invisible when turned sideways (3 rounds + 1 round/level).
*   **Limited Wish (Conj.):** Fulfills literally, but only partially or for a limited duration, the utterance of the spell caster. Will not substantially change major realities.
*   **Mass Invisibility (Phantasm):** Makes up to 300-400 man-sized creatures invisible within a 3" X 3" area.
*   **Monster Summoning V (Conj.):** Summons 1-2 fifth level monsters.
*   **Mordenkainen's Sword (Evoc.):** Brings into being a shimmering sword-like plane of force that strikes as a fighter of half the caster's level, dealing 5-20 HP damage.
*   **Phase Door (Alt.):** Creates an invisible door/passage through a wall for the caster to enter and disappear (lasts 1 usage/2 levels).
*   **Power Word, Stun (Conj.):** Stuns one creature with up to 90 HP for 1-8 rounds, with duration depending on the victim's current HP.
*   **Reverse Gravity (Alt.):** Reverses gravity in a 1/2"/level range area, causing all unfixed objects and creatures to "fall" upwards.
*   **Simulacrum (Phantasm):** Creates a non-material duplicate of any creature, with 51-60% of the original's HP and some knowledge/personality (formed from snow/ice).
*   **Statue:** Turns the caster or another creature (and gear) into solid stone (subject to save), where they can still see/hear/smell.
*   **Teleport Without Error:** Instantly transports the caster and additional weight to a well-known destination without any risk of error.

#### EIGHTH LEVEL

*   **Antipathy/Sympathy (Charm):** Creates certain vibrations that either repel (*Antipathy*) or attract (*Sympathy*) a specific type of living, intelligent creature or character alignment in the area/item.
*   **Bigby's Clenched Fist (Evoc.):** Creates a huge, disembodied hand of force that strikes an opponent each round, with varying degrees of damage/stunning (1-6 HP up to 4-24 HP).
*   **Clone (Nec.):** Creates a duplicate of a person, complete to the level of experience, which takes 2-8 months to grow. Dual existence of original and clone causes insanity/suicide.

*   **Incendiary Cloud (Evoc.):** Creates a dense vapor cloud (10' high, 20' long/wide) that begins to flame on the 3rd round, dealing 1/2 HP damage/level to creatures inside.
*   **Mass Charm (Charm):** Charms a number of creatures (total HD not exceeding twice the caster's level) in a maximum 3" X 3" area.
*   **Maze (Conj.):** Traps one creature in an extra-dimensional shifting labyrinth of force planes. The creature wanders for a period dependent on its intelligence.
*   **Mind Blank (Abj.):** Totally protects the recipient from all spells and devices that detect, influence, or read emotions/thoughts (e.g., *charm, ESP, geas, wish*).
*   **Monster Summoning VI (Conj.):** Summons 1 or 2 sixth level monsters.

*   **Serten's Spell Immunity (Abj.):** Confers virtual immunity and a large bonus (+5 to +9) to saving throws against certain spells (e.g., *charm, fear, hold, geas*).
*   **Symbol (Conj.):** Same as Cleric 7th Level, with different possible effects (Death, Discord, Fear, Hopelessness).

#### NINTH LEVEL

*   **Bigby's Crushing Hand (Evoc.):** Creates a huge, disembodied hand of force that grasps and squeezes an opponent, dealing increasing damage over successive rounds.

### ILLUSIONIST SPELLS (Magic-Users)

*Note: Many Illusionist spells are similar to Magic-User spells of the same name, but often with minor differences in range, area, or components. Some key unique illusions are highlighted.*

#### FIRST LEVEL

*   **Audible Glamer (Phantasm):** Creates a volume of sound (talking, singing, roaring, etc.) at whatever range the caster desires (up to a dragon's roar).
*   **Change Self (Phantasm):** Alters the appearance of the illusionist's form, including clothing and equipment, to any generally man-shaped bipedal creature.
*   **Color Spray (Alt.):** Causes a vivid, fan-shaped spray of colors that affects 1-6 creatures, potentially rendering them unconscious, blinded, or stunned, based on their HD/level.
*   **Gaze Reflection (Alt.):** Creates a mirror-like area of air that reflects back any gaze attack (e.g., basilisk, medusa) upon the gazer.
*   **Phantasmal Force (Phantasm):** Creates a visual illusion (same as M-U 1st Level, but the illusionist maintains the illusion with minimal concentration in *Improved Phantasmal Force*).
*   **Wall Of Fog (Alt.):** Creates a wall of misty vapors that obscures all sight (normal or infravision) beyond 2'.

#### SECOND LEVEL

*   **Blindness (Phantasm):** Causes the recipient creature to become totally blind (no cure except *dispel magic* or caster's will).
*   **Blur (Phantasm):** Causes the outline of the illusionist's form to become blurred, shifting, and wavery, penalizing missile and melee attacks against them.
*   **Hypnotic Pattern (Phantasm):** Creates a weaving, turning pattern of subtle colors that causes creatures looking at it to become fascinated and entranced (up to 24 HD/levels total).
*   **Improved Phantasmal Force (Phantasm):** A phantasmal force illusion that can be maintained with minimal concentration (lasts 2 rounds after concentration ceases).
*   **Invisibility:** Same as M-U 2nd Level.

#### THIRD LEVEL

*   **Dispel Illusion (Abj.):** Dispels any *phantasmal force* (with or without audible glamer) cast by a non-illusionist, and has a chance to dispel other illusionist spells.
*   **Illusionary Script (Phantasm):** Creates illusionary script on paper/parchment that can only be read by the person(s) the illusionist desires (others become confused).
*   **Invisibility, 10' Radius (Phantasm):** Same as M-U 3rd Level.
*   **Paralyzation (Phantasm):** Creates illusionary muscle slowdown that paralyzes creatures whose total HD do not exceed twice the illusionist's level.
*   **Spectral Force (Phantasm):** Creates an illusion that includes sound, smell, and thermal illusions.

#### FOURTH LEVEL

*   **Dispel Exhaustion (Phantasm):** Gives the illusion that a touched person is fresh and well, restoring 50% of lost HP.
*   **Emotion (Charm):** Projects a chosen emotion (Fear, Hate, Hopelessness, Rage) from the illusionist, affecting morale, attack rolls, damage, or saving throws.
*   **Phantasmal Killer (Phantasm):** Creates the illusion of the victim's most fearsome imagined thing. On a successful hit, the victim dies from fright.

#### FIFTH LEVEL

*   **Chaos (Charm):** Same as M-U 5th Level.
*   **Demi-Shadow Monsters (Phantasm):** Creates quasi-real monsters (40% real) of armor class 8 with 40% of normal hit points and damage potential.
*   **Major Creation (Alt.):** Creates mineral objects (same as *Minor Creation* for vegetable objects, but permanent).
*   **Maze (Conj.):** Same as M-U 8th Level.
*   **Shadow Magic (Phantasm):** Allows the caster to cast a quasi-real M-U spell (*magic missile, fireball, lightning bolt, or cone of cold*) that deals damage (or 1 HP/level if recognized).

#### SIXTH LEVEL

*   **Demi-Shadow Magic (Phantasm):** Allows the caster to cast quasi-real M-U spells (*wall of fire, wall of ice, or cloudkill*) that deal more damage than *Shadow Magic*.
*   **Mass Suggestion (Charm):** Same as M-U 3rd Level, but affects one creature per level of the illusionist.
*   **Permanent Illusion (Phantasm):** Creates a lasting *spectral force* (same as *Phantasmal Force* but permanent and requires no concentration).
*   **Shades (Phantasm):** Creates quasi-real monsters (60% real) of armor class 6 with 60% of normal hit points and damage potential.
*   **True Sight (Div.):** Allows the illusionist to see its actual or former form, but does not allow determination of alignment.
*   **Veil (Phantasm):** Instantly changes the appearance of surroundings (up to 2" X 2"/level) to fool creatures, making a filthy den look like a sumptuous room.

#### SEVENTH LEVEL

*   **Alter Reality (Phantasm, Conj.):** Similar to *Limited Wish*, allowing the illusionist to alter reality through the casting of a *phantasmal force* illusion.
*   **Prismatic Spray (Abj., Conj.):** Causes 7 rays of the *prismatic sphere* spell (red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, violet) to spring from the caster's hand, affecting creatures with a random effect (based on d8 roll).
*   **Prismatic Wall (Abj., Conj.):** Creates a wall, or curtain, of scintillating colors with effects identical to the *prismatic sphere* globes, with maximum proportions 4' wide per level and 2' high per level.
*   **Vision (Div.):** Allows the illusionist to call upon a power for supernatural guidance, receiving a *vision* in answer to a question (chance of success based on dice roll, intelligence, and sacrifice).
