### Dwarf Special Abilities

*   **Class Limits:** Fighter (max 9), Thief (U), Assassin (max 10), or Fighter/Thief (Multiclassing is subject to thief armor/weapon limits when acting as a thief, and XP is always divided equally).
*   **Saving Throw Bonus:** +1 for every 3.5 points of Constitution (rounded down) vs. magic wands, staves, rods, and spells, as well as vs. poison. (Max +5 at CON 18+).
*   **Infravision:** 60' in the dark.
*   **Mining Skill (Detects when within 10'):** Detect grade/slope (75%), new construction/passage (75%), sliding/shifting walls/rooms (66 2/3%), traps in stone (50%), approx. depth underground (50%).
*   **Combat:** +1 to hit half-orcs, goblins, hobgoblins, orcs. -4 AC penalty to opponents who are ogres, trolls, ogre magi, giants, and/or titans.
*   **Charisma Max:** 16/16 for own race.

### Elf Special Abilities

*   **Class Limits:** Fighter (max 7), Magic-User (max 11), Thief (U), Assassin (max 10), or Multi-Class combinations (F/MU, F/T, MU/T, F/MU/T). Multiclass XP always divided equally.
*   **Resistance:** 90% resistance to *Sleep* and *Charm* spells.
*   **Combat Bonus:** +1 "to hit" with bows (non-crossbow) or short/long swords.
*   **Infravision:** 60' in darkness.
*   **Secret Door Detection:** 16 2/3% (1 in 6) chance by merely passing within 10'. 33 1/3% (2 in 6) when actively searching. 50% (3 in 6) to detect concealed doors when actively searching.
*   **Surprise:** 66 2/3% (d6, 1-4) chance to surprise when alone and not in metal armor (or 90' in advance of a party without metal armor), unless a portal must be opened (then 33 1/3% chance).

### Gnome Special Abilities

*   **Class Limits:** Fighter (max 6), Illusionist (max 7), Thief (U), Assassin (max 8), or Multi-Class combinations (F/I, F/T, I/T). Multiclass XP always divided equally.
*   **Saving Throw Bonus:** +1 for every 3.5 points of Constitution (rounded down) vs. magic wands, staves, rods, and spells (Max +5 at CON 18+).
*   **Infravision:** 60' in darkness.
*   **Mining Skill (Detects when within 10'):** Detect grade/slope (80%), unsafe walls/ceilings/floors (70%), approx. depth underground (60%), direction of travel underground (50%).
*   **Combat:** +1 to hit kobolds or goblins. -4 AC penalty to opponents who are gnolls, bugbears, ogres, trolls, ogre magi, giants, and/or titans.

### Half-Elf Special Abilities

*   **Class Limits:** Cleric (max 5), Druid (U), Fighter (max 8), Ranger (max 8), Magic-User (max 8), Thief (U), Assassin (max 11), or Multi-Class combinations (C/F, C/R, C/MU, F/MU, F/T, MU/T, C/F/MU, F/MU/T). Multiclass XP always divided equally.
*   **Resistance:** 30% resistance to *Sleep* and *Charm* spells.
*   **Infravision:** 60' in darkness.
*   **Secret Door Detection:** 16 2/3% chance by merely passing within 10'. 33 1/3% (2 in 6) when actively searching. 50% (3 in 6) to detect concealed doors when actively searching.

### Halfling Special Abilities

*   **Class Limits:** Fighter (max 6), Thief (U), or Fighter/Thief (Multiclassing XP always divided equally).
*   **Resistance:** +1 for every 3.5 points of Constitution (rounded down) vs. magic wands, staves, rods, spells, and poison (Max +5 at CON 18+).
*   **Infravision:** Pure Stoutish blood up to 60', Mixed blood up to 30'.
*   **Passage/Direction Detection:** Note up/down grade (75%), determine direction (50%).
*   **Silent Movement:** 66 2/3% (d6, 1-4) chance to surprise when alone (or 90' in advance of a party without metal armor), unless a door must be opened (then 33 1/3% chance).

### Half-Orc Special Abilities

*   **Class Limits:** Cleric (max 4), Fighter (max 10), Thief (U), Assassin (U), or Multi-Class combinations (C/F, C/T, C/A, F/T, F/A). Multiclass XP always divided equally.
*   **Infravision:** 60' in darkness.
*   **Racial Penalty:** Charisma -2 (only for non-orc henchmen).

### Human Special Abilities

*   **Class Limits:** Unlimited level advancement in any class (Cleric, Druid, Fighter, Paladin, Ranger, Magic-User, Illusionist, Thief, Assassin, Monk).
*   **No Racial Penalties or Bonuses:** Established as the norm.
