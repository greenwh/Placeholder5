**Summary Description:** A cleric is a dedicated combatant and spellcaster (d8 HD), whose primary attribute is **Wisdom (min 9)**. They gain a 10% XP bonus for WIS 16+. They serve a deity and are forbidden to use edged/pointed weapons that shed blood (e.g., swords, spears), but can wear any armor and use a shield. Their spells are *bestowed* by their deity and focus on protection, revitalization, and limited offense. A key ability is the power to **Turn or Command Undead** (and certain demons/devils). Upon reaching 8th level (Patriarch/Matriarch), a cleric attracts **20-200 followers** and can establish a place of worship for monthly revenue.

**Summary Description:** The druid is the sole **True Neutral (N)** subclass of cleric (d8 HD). They have a minimum **Wisdom (min 12) and Charisma (min 15)**, gaining a 10% XP bonus if both exceed 15. They worship nature, are restricted to **Leather Armor and Wooden Shields** (metal spoils their magic), and use nature-attuned spells. They cannot turn undead.

*   **Role & Alignment:** Cleric sub-class (d8 HD) who must be **True Neutral (N)**, maintaining the balance of nature (viewing law and chaos, good and evil, as necessary balancing forces).
*   **Ability Requirements:** Requires high scores: **WIS min 12, CHA min 15**. Gains 10% XP bonus if both exceed 15.
*   **Combat:** Fights as a **Cleric**. Makes saving throws as a Cleric, but gains a +2 bonus against **fire and lightning** attacks.
*   **Armor/Weapons:** Restricted to **Leather Armor** and **Wooden Shields** (no metallic armor/shields). Restricted to a list of non-edged weapons (e.g., club, scimitar, hammer).
*   **Spellcasting:** Uses unique spells (Druidic Spells) attuned to nature and the outdoors. Cannot **Turn or Control Undead**, demons, or devils.
*   **Innate Powers (3rd Lvl - Initiate of the 1st Circle):**
    *   Identify plant/animal type and pure water.
    *   Pass through overgrown areas without leaving a trail.
*   **Innate Powers (7th Lvl - Initiate of the 5th Circle):**
    *   Immunity from woodland *charm* spells (dryads, nixies, etc.).
    *   **Change Form** up to three times per day (mammal, reptile, or bird, size limited).
*   **Advancement & Hierarchy:** The Druid class is limited to a strict hierarchy:
    *   Only **nine** Druids (12th level) can exist.
    *   To advance to **Druid (12th)**, the character must defeat one of the nine existing Druids in spell or hand-to-hand combat (loser drops to the lowest possible XP for the next lower level).
    *   The same process is repeated to become one of the three **Archdruids** (13th level), and then **The Great Druid** (14th level), who is the sole supreme druid.
*   **Stronghold/Dwelling:** Prefers to live in sacred groves or natural settings, avoiding castles, cities, and towns.

#### PALADIN SUMMARY DESCRIPTION

**Summary Description:** A paladin is a sub-class of fighter (d10 HD) who must be **Human** and strictly **Lawful Good** with exceptional ability scores (**STR min 12, INT min 9, WIS min 13, CON min 9, CHA min 17**). They gain a 10% XP bonus if STR and WIS exceed 15. They have heavy restrictions on wealth and magic item possession.


*   **Role & Alignment:** Fighter sub-class (d10 HD) who must be **Human** and start/remain **Lawful Good (LG)**. Immediately loses all special benefits if a knowing and willing **evil act** is performed. Requires seeking a high-level lawful good cleric and performing **penance** if a chaotic act is performed.
*   **Ability Requirements:** High minimum scores are essential: **STR 12, INT 9, WIS 13, CON 9, CHA 17**. Gains 10% XP bonus if STR and WIS exceed 15.
*   **Combat:** Fights on the **Fighter Attack Table**. Gains 3 attacks per 2 rounds at level 7-12.
*   **Special Powers:**
    1.  **Detect Evil:** Up to 60' distance, as often as desired.
    2.  **Saving Throw Bonus:** +2 on all saving throws.
    3.  **Immunity:** Immune to all forms of disease.
    4.  **Lay On Hands:** Cures 2 HP damage per level of experience once per day.
    5.  **Cure Disease:** Can cure 1 disease per week for each five levels of experience.
    6.  **Aura of Protection:** Continuous *protection from evil* in a 1" radius.
    7.  **Turn Undead/Devils/Demons (3rd Level):** Affects as if a 1st level Cleric, increasing in power with each level.
    8.  **Warhorse (4th Level):** Can magically call for an **intelligent heavy warhorse**. Only one available every 10 years if lost.
    9.  **Holy Sword:** Projects a circle of *dispel magic* power (equal to the paladin's level) when holding a special *Holy Sword*.
    10. **Cleric Spells (9th Level):** Gains limited Cleric spell ability (up to 4th level).
*   **Restrictions:**
    *   **Wealth:** Must give away excess wealth and tithe 10% of all income to a lawful good religious institution.
    *   **Magic Items:** May never retain more than **ten magic items** (including only 1 armor suit, 1 shield, and 4 weapons).
    *   **Association:** Associates only with characters and creatures of **Good** alignment. Adventuring companies may only contain non-evil neutrals on a single expedition basis.
    *   **Henchmen:** Can only have lawful good henchmen.

#### RANGER SUMMARY DESCRIPTION

**Summary Description:** A ranger is a sub-class of fighter (d8 HD, starts with 2d8 at 1st level) specializing in woodcraft, tracking, and scouting. They must be of **Good** alignment (lawful, chaotic, or neutral). They have high ability score minimums (**STR min 13, INT min 13, WIS min 14, CON min 14**) and gain a 10% XP bonus if STR, INT, and WIS all exceed 15.

*   **Role & Alignment:** Fighter sub-class (starts with 2d8 HD, 1d8/level thereafter) who must be of **Good** alignment (lawful, chaotic, or neutral). If alignment changes to non-Good, all benefits are stripped, and the character becomes a Fighter.
*   **Ability Requirements:** Requires high scores: **STR min 13, INT min 13, WIS min 14, CON min 14**. Gains 10% XP bonus if STR, INT, and WIS all exceed 15.
*   **Combat:** Fights on the **Fighter Attack Table**. Gains 3 attacks per 2 rounds at level 8-14.
    *   **Giant Class Bonus:** Adds **+1 HP for each level** of experience to damage scored in melee combat against "giant class" creatures (bugbears, giants, ogres, trolls, etc.).
*   **Special Powers:**
    1.  **Surprise:** Surprises opponents **50%** of the time. Is surprised only **16 2/3%** of the time.
    2.  **Tracking:** Has a base **90%** chance to track outdoors, modified by creature number, time elapsed (-10%/24 hours), and precipitation (-25%/hour). Base 65% chance underground.
    3.  **Druidic Spells (8th Level):** Gains limited Druidic spell ability (up to 3rd level).
    4.  **Magic-User Spells (9th Level):** Gains limited Magic-User spell ability (up to 2nd level). Cannot read spells from magic scrolls.
    5.  **Magic Item Use (10th Level):** Can employ all non-written magic items pertaining to *clairaudience, clairvoyance, ESP, and telepathy*.
    6.  **Followers (10th Level):** Attracts 2-24 followers (once lost, they cannot be replaced).
*   **Restrictions:**
    *   **Wealth:** May only own goods/treasure they can carry on their person or mount; all excess must be **donated** to a worthy communal or institutional cause.
    *   **Association:** No more than **three** rangers may ever operate together.
    *   **Henchmen:** May not hire men-at-arms, servants, or henchmen until they attain **8th or higher level**.

#### MONK SUMMARY DESCRIPTION

**Summary Description:** The monk is a rare and difficult class (d4 HD, starts with d4+d4 at 1st level), requiring extremely high minimum ability scores (**STR min 15, WIS min 15, DEX min 15, CON min 11**). They must be **Lawful** alignment (good, neutral, or evil). They are highly restricted in armor and weapon use.


*   **Role & Alignment:** Monastic aesthetic (d4 HD, starts with 2d4 HD) who must start/remain **Lawful** (Good, Neutral, or Evil).
*   **Ability Requirements:** Requires exceptional scores: **STR min 15, WIS min 15, DEX min 15, CON min 11**. No XP bonus for high scores. No AC adjustment from Dexterity.
*   **Combat:** Fights with bare hands on the **Thief Attack Table**.
    *   **Damage Bonus:** Adds **1/2 HP per level** to open hand damage.
    *   **Open Hand:** Damage and attacks per round increase with level (see table).
    *   **Stun/Kill:** Chance to **stun** (if "to hit" roll exceeds AC required by 5+) or **kill** (percentage chance equals AC of stunned opponent + levels over 7th) on a successful hit and stun.
*   **Thief Abilities:** Performs **6 thief abilities** (*Open Locks, Find/Remove Traps, Move Silently, Hide in Shadows, Hear Noise, Climb Walls*) at a chance **identical** to a thief of the same level.
*   **Special Powers (from Table II):**
    *   **A (3rd Lvl):** Speak with animals.
    *   **B (4th Lvl):** ESP 30% chance of success vs. monk. Can escape falling damage (up to 20').
    *   **C (5th Lvl):** Immune to disease, *haste*, and *slow* spells.
    *   **D (6th Lvl):** Catalepsy (feign death).
    *   **E (7th Lvl):** Heal 1d4+1 HP/day (on self).
    *   **F (8th Lvl):** Speak with plants.
    *   **G (9th Lvl):** 50% resistance to *Beguiling, Charms, Hypnosis, Suggestion* spells.
    *   **H (10th Lvl):** Mental discipline causes Telepathic/Mind Blast attacks to be made as if the monk had 18 INT.
    *   **K (13th Lvl):** Can fall any distance if within 8' of a wall.
*   **Restrictions:**
    *   **Armor/Weapons:** Cannot wear armor or use a shield. Restricted to a short list of weapons.
    *   **Wealth:** May retain only two magic weapons and three other magic items. Must bestow all other treasure on a religious institution.
    *   **Advancement:** Past 7th level, advancement requires **finding and defeating** a higher-level monk in unarmed combat, or losing experience.
    *   **Combat Focus:** Must use *Monk* proficiency when attacking with bare hands; does not benefit from strength bonuses for "to hit" or damage.
    *   **Quivering Palm (16th Lvl):** Fabled attack to set up vibrations causing death (limited use, subject to creature HD and save).
